Adult Classes

The Adult Program at Old City Aikido is both comprehensive and dynamic. A strong foundation in weapons training as well as our fluid style of ukemi, gives students the tools they will need to practice Aikido at the highest level. Seasoned Aikido practitioners as well as new students are always welcome to come and practice!

Visit Us

Carefully hidden away within the heart of Philadelphia's historic Downtown district, Old City Aikido offers all the convenience of the big city while preserving the more reserved nature of a traditional dojo. Visitors will be engaged by a helpful senior staff and will find our dojo community to be welcoming and ready to train!
